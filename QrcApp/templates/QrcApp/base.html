EDUCATION PROGRAMS

Trailblazer Education Fund (TEF Program)

This fund is designed to award scholarships to deserving young boys and girls who exhibit great intelligence and desire to enhance their education but are emanating from a vulnerable home or community and finances are standing as a stumbling block to their educational achievements. This fund will seek to support such young brilliant minds with financial support for their tuition fee and procurement of learning materials.

COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAMS

Trash To Treasure

This program emanated from a youth resolution during a climate change awareness training we conducted on 11th December, 2015 in Bauleni Township. The idea is to train youth ambassadors in techniques of reusing, recycling and reducing waste; who in turn will be responsible to train other young people in their communities the techniques of reusing, recycling, and reducing waste. The proposal was that this program be merged with some aspects of entrepreneurship where the youth would see the huge problem of waste they face in their communities as an opportunity for economic growth.
